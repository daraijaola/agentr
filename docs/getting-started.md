# Getting Started with AGENTR

AGENTR is a hosted platform — no installation, no setup, no technical knowledge required. This guide gets you from zero to a live AI agent in under 2 minutes.

---

## 1. Sign Up

Go to [agentr.online](https://agentr.online) and click **Launch your agent**.

Enter your Telegram phone number. You'll receive a verification code in your Telegram app.

> No password required. No email. Just your Telegram number.

---

## 2. Verify

Enter the 5-digit code sent to your Telegram app. If you have Telegram 2FA enabled, enter your cloud password when prompted.

---

## 3. Set Up Your Agent

Give your agent a name and tell it what to call you. Choose who can trigger it — everyone, contacts only, or just yourself.

---

## 4. Pick a Plan

Start with the **free 24-hour trial** — no credit card required. Upgrade anytime from the dashboard.

---

## 5. Your Agent Is Live

That's it. Open Telegram and message your agent account. Talk to it in plain English.

```
You: "Build me a TON price tracker and host it"
Agent: [writes code, deploys it, sends you the live link]

You: "Create a Telegram channel called TON News and post to it daily"
Agent: [creates the channel, sets it up, schedules the posts]

You: "Check my TON wallet balance and swap 1 TON for USDT on DeDust"
Agent: [checks balance, gets quote, asks confirmation, executes swap]
```

---

## What Your Agent Can Do

- **Build and deploy** — write code, deploy apps, create Telegram bots, serve websites
- **TON blockchain** — send TON, swap tokens, manage .ton domains, check balances
- **Telegram automation** — manage chats, send media, schedule tasks, handle contacts
- **Agent Swarm** — spawn parallel sub-agents for complex multi-step tasks
- **Persistent memory** — remembers facts across every conversation

---

## Dashboard

At [agentr.online](https://agentr.online) you can:

- **Workspace** — edit your agent's personality (SOUL), rules (STRATEGY), and memory (MEMORY.md)
- **Marketplace** — deploy community-built agent configurations in one click
- **Bots & Processes** — see what your agent has deployed and view logs
- **Activity** — see recent tasks your agent completed
- **Credits** — top up with TON via TON Connect
